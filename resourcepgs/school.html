Back to school!

Epic hires smart, ambitious people. It's no surprise, then, that many choose to go back to school after their tenure. Some of the post-Epic academic pursuits I've witnessed include:

Healthcare

  • Medical school (MD/DO)
  • Nursing school (RN/BSN/MSN)
  • Chiropractic school (DC)
  • Social Work (MSW)
  • Healthcare/Health Services Administration (MHA)
  • Healthcare Informatics
  • Public Health (MPH)

Non-Healthcare

  • PhD work in many fields
  • Law school (JD)
  • Business school (MBA)
  • Computer Science (MSCS)
  • Music (Master of Music)
  • Accounting (Master of Accountancy and/or 150 hours needed for CPA)

Testing, testing: 1, 2, 3

The program you're interested in will determine the required test(s) that must accompany your application package. Even then, one school might want or accept a different test than another for the same program.
