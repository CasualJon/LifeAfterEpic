Back to school!

Epic hires smart, ambitious people. It's no surprise, then, that many choose to go back to school after their tenure. Some of the post-Epic academic pursuits I've witnessed include:

Healthcare

  • Medical school (MD/DO)
  • Nursing school (RN/BSN/MSN)
  • Chiropractic school (DC)
  • Social Work (MSW)
  • Healthcare/Health Services Administration (MHA)

Non-Healthcare

  • Law school (JD)
  • Business school (MBA)
  • Computer Science (MSCS)
  • Music (Master of Music)
  • Accounting (Master of Accountancy and/or 150 hours needed for CPA)

Testing, testing: 1, 2, 3

The program you choose will determine the required test(s) that must accompany your application package. Even so, one school might want or accept a different test than another school for the same program.

  • MCAT: Medical College Admission Test

  • TEAS: Test of Essential Academic Skills

  • LSAT: Law School Admission Test
    • Specifically designed for law school admissions, and required by most American Bar Association-approved law schools
    • Starting with 2018-2019 application cycle, some schools are beginning to accept the GRE as an alternative
    • ~3 hours: Reading Comprehension, Analytical Reasoning, Logical Reasoning (2 sections), Writing Sample (unscored)
    • Owned by LSAC (Law School Admission Council)

  • GMAT: Graduate Management Admission Test
    • Specifically designed for graduate business and management programs
    • ~3.5 hours: Analytical Writing (1 essay), Integrated Reasoning, Verbal Reasoning, Quantative Reasoning
    • Owned by GMAC (Graduate Management Admission Council)

  • GRE: Graduate Record Exam
    • GRE General Test
      • Multi-purpose test for graduate admissions
      • ~3 hours: Analytical Writing (2 essays), Verbal Reasoning (2 sections), Quantative Reasoning (2 sections)
      • Owned by ETS (Educational Testing Service)
    • GRE Subject Tests
      • Biology
      • Chemistry
      • Literature in English
      • Mathematics
      • Physics
      • Psychology

  • TOEFL: Test Of English as a Foreign Language
