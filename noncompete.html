Epic Non-Compete


If you're interested in consulting, you have a year to burn.

Let Consultants Know When You're Available

To ease navigation into this arena, since consulting groups are not supposed to be in contact with you before your non-compete is up, we can let the right people know when your window is set to expire.


If you like, we're also happy to let local recruiters know of your availability and timelines.


Keep in mind that if you purchased any stock options (which replaced SARs awards in starting in 2017), then your non-compete period was extended by the agreement signed.
